Scrapbox features

All the features you need to make writing and exploring ideas faster than ever.

Bi-directional links

Files and folders become hard to use at a certain scale and complexity. In Scrapbox, any words you type in [brackets] or with a #hashtag become bi-directional links instantly. It's a linked knowledge graph that's easy to use at any scale.

Simple editing, all together

Start typing on a line and simple markup is revealed such as brackets for links. As soon as you move to the next line the styled result is shown. Working together on a page is just as easy with simple version control to handle any conflicts.

Paste to embed

Copy and paste images, screenshots, Google Maps, and Youtube videos or drag and drop them on the page. Everything displays inline so you can make abstract concepts easier to understand in seconds. Simply paste and keep typing.

Security

Scrapbox is trusted by leading companies, cutting-edge research labs, and a wide range of makers and thinkers. Safeguarding your data and privacy is core to our values.

256-bit SSL encryption

All transfers are TLS / SSL encrypted so it's functionally impossible for your data to be intercepted by third parties.

Privacy by default

Every Scrapbox project is fully private and its content is only visible to invited members, unless the creator or an admin makes it public.

Strong authentication support

Use Google or G Suite SSO authentication in the cloud or get the on-premise verison for email auth, Azure AD, and GitHub Enterprise.

World-class data centers

We manage and store data on Heroku, a renowned cloud service provider with extremely high reliability and security.

External security inspection

We update Scrapbox based on regular testing results from a vulnerability discovery platform that has customers including the US Department of Defense.

Solid track record

Our web services have been trusted by leading companies and researchers around the world for over a decade.
